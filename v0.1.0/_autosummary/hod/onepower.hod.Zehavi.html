onepower.hod.Zehavi

class onepower.hod.Zehavi(cosmo=None, mass=None, dndlnm=None, halo_bias=None, z_vec=None, hod_settings=None, **model_parameters)[source]

HOD model from Zehavi et al. (2004) [1]_.

Same as Zheng model in the limit that sigma=0 and M0=0 Mean number of central galaxies is only ever 0 or 1 in this HOD
